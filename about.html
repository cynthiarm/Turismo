about Rocky Point - Arizona Trade & Tourism Office

We are a private sector trade and tourism office, focused on creating and putting togheter opportunities across, through facilitating information and professional advice from our staff on immigration requirements, commercial regulations and general information that will guide you to set up your business.

Services to US residents

Immigration Advise

In México there are regulations for citizens of other countries living here. There is an Immigration office in town and you can do your filing online at www.inami.gob.mx or you can do it through an immigration consultant, who will guide through the application process

Emergency Assistance

If you are in an emergency, call 911. If you have any questions around issues with the Mexican authorities, you can contact us and our staff will assist you. If you need an attorney, we will help you with recommending the professional legal providers.

City Directory

If you need to consult the city directory, you can access the following link to the official directory of Puerto Peñasco http://puertopenasco.gob.mx/

Opening A Business

Planning or working on setting up a bussiness in Rocky Point? We can help you, get in contact with us at info@azrptradetourism.com
