History of Rocky Point

Puerto Peñasco known as Rocky Point, is a Mexican city located in the northwest of the state of Sonora, in the area of ​​the great desert of Altar,it is known as a city of resorts and fishing in the Gulf of California, for the Sandy beach, with sand dunes, and the tide pools of La Choya Bay.


In the CetMar aquarium there are turtles and seals, while the skeleton of a whale is exhibited in the Intercultural Center for Desert and Oceans Studies, located nearby. To the northwest, the El Pinacate Biosphere Reserve and the Great Altar Desert encompass volcanic and desert terrain.


Discovery

In the year of 1826, a retired lieutenant of the British Royal Navy called Robert William Hale Hardy sailed in this area of ​​the Gulf of California in search of pearls and precious metals, in his trip he found the coast of the gulf and the desert of this place, and observed a hill that jutted to the shore, (the hill of the Whale), by its geography called "Rocky Point" to this place and under that name began to be identified in the marine maps of the explorers.
In the 1920s, the area began to be used temporarily as a small secure fishing ground for residents of nearby places, who used it to fish for totoaba, a very coveted fish at that time and that currently its fishing is closed. its danger of extinction. The site was known as "Punta de Piedra".


In 1928, two men named John Stone and Sofus Jhanssen from Ajo, Arizona, came here to build a hotel / casino and take advantage of people coming across the border escaping the US ban on alcohol. They drilled a 21-meter deep water well, and set up a service called "Scenic Airlines" for flights by plane from Phoenix and Tucson to attract tourists to drink, play and fish. The hotel was called "Marine Club", which the present is called "Hotel La Roca", and it is an emblematic building of the city because of its stone construction, which was brought from the hill of the whale and cut in blocks by craftsmen, almost uniformly equal in size and shape. It is said that Al Capone frequented the place.


In the year of 1930 the president of the country Lázaro Cárdenas named this place "Punta Peñasco", this due to the shape of the tip on the sea shore and the rock formation on that shore, a hill of volcanic origin and from which cliffs stand out of solidified lava that enter the ocean. In the 1930s, the construction of a railroad line was started to connect the Baja California peninsula with the rest of Mexico, passing through Puerto Peñasco. The small fishing camp began to be inhabited by people who came to work in the construction of this railway, In 1936, President Cárdenas's delegation received the promise to unite Baja California with the rest of the country, devising the construction of a dock to receive and ship goods and materials on trains. of cargo that will arrive to the dock, and transfer them to the city of Mexicali and vice versa.


On July 9, 1952, through the law number 136, the municipality of Puerto Peñasco was created. The city was separated from the municipality of Caborca, with the localities of Sonoyta, La Choya, 21 de Marzo and Cuauhtémoc remaining under its new territory. In 1972, the president of the republic, Luis Echeverría Álvarez, delivered to the fishing sector of the community works of port and fishing infrastructure, consolidating with it the main economic activity. In September of 1989, the municipal surface undergoes a reduction of its territory when being created in the state the municipality number 70, General Plutarco Elías Calles and the town of Sonoyta.


The tourist development was impelled as of year 2000 with a growth of 7.34% index that was over the average state and national growth.


Weather

Puerto Peñasco has a dry-semi-warm climate with a maximum monthly average temperature of 27.5 ° C in the months of July and August and a minimum monthly average of 17.5 ° C in December and January the average annual temperature is 22.5 ° C. The rainy season occurs in the summer in the months of July and August with an average annual precipitation of 66.0 millimeters.


Demography

According to the results of the 2010 Population and Housing Census of the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI), the city of Puerto Peñasco has a total of 56,756 inhabitants, which makes it the ninth largest city in the state.

Economy

Fishing

Due to its great potential in marine resources, one of the main economic activities of the city is fishing and commercialization, for which it has a large fishing fleet and several processing companies. The main fish and seafood are shrimp, chano, crab, saw and dogfish. The fishing fleet of height that counts is of 125 larger boats and 300 smaller boats for fishing on the riverbank. The volumes of capture have made Sonora occupy the first places at national level, both for national consumption and for export.


Tourism

Tourism is the main economic support of the city, with about 2,500,000 visitors per year, contributed a lot to develop the infrastructure of hotels, condominiums, restaurants and recreational centers, in addition to trade and services of the industry. It has stood out for hosting constant water and sports events such as sailing, motorcycling, off road racing, motorcycling in sand, diving, among others.


Commerce

The commercial activity is one of its most important activities, since there are around 706 different businesses, among which are the companies related to tourism services. The trade is subdivided into more than 40 types and is made up of hotels, restaurants, gas stations, workshops, groceries, self-service and craft shopping centers.


Real State

The real estate sector has been on the rise since the 2000s, due to domestic and foreign investment which has driven hotel and residential projects, with its economic demand linked to the US and Canadian markets. From 2000 to 2004 this Port already had approximately 2,095 residential homes, 921 condominiums distributed, accounting for a total of 1,953 rooms. In 2005, the economic income of this sector reached 303,929 million pesos, placing Puerto Peñasco in one of the most productive cities in the area. In 2007, 60 projects were presented in the city. real estate construction already registered.
