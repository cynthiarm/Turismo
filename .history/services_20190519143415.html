Services to US residents

Inmigration Advise

In Mexico there are regulations for citizens of other countries living here. Most of the time US citizens do not stop to get their tourist permit at the border and come directly to Rocky Point, as RP is considered a tourist destination.
If you are visiting Rocky Point for business we advise you to get your immigration document at the border.Mexican immigration law offers two categories for US citizens living in the country, as temporary or permanent residents.
Both require a set of requirements and fees incur. There is an Immigration office in town and you can do your filing online at www.inami.gob.mx or you can do it through an immigration consultant, who will guide through the process of filing.
If you have questions regarding this, you can contact us.

Emergency Assistance

In you are in an emergency, call 911. If you have any questions around issues with the Mexican authorities, you can contact us and our staff will assist you with information.
If you need an attorney, also you can contact us and we will help you with recommending the professional legal providers in town that can help you in your situation.
