Economy

The economy of the port is supported by different important sectors

Fishing & Food Processing

When it comes to fishing and recreation activities in the sea, there are a vast list of options, from scuba diving, whale watching, educational tours or fishing trips on a safe and tourist friendly companies.
Sea food processing is a business growing also, with companies exporting to Asia and US markets. The Gulf of California has been recognized for the quality of its products. We are a private sector trade and tourism office, focused on creating and putting togheter opportunities across, through facilitating information and professional advice from our staff on immigration requirements, commercial regulations and general information that will guide you to set up your business.


Shrimp & Fish

Puerto Peñasco is one of the top 3 cities in Sonora that exports a diversity of shrimp and fish such as shrimp that is ranked as one of the most delicious shrimp in Mexico, their size varies from U8, U10, U12, U15, U16, U20, U21, as the number decreases the bigger shrimp you will receive. Most of the local companies export their products to San Diego and Los Angeles California. 3 of the most popular fish to export is flounder, gulf coney, grouper.


Jellyfish

The cannon ball fishing exceeded 60 thousand tons in 2018, says Marco Antonio Ross Guerrero, Undersecretary of Fisheries and Aquaculture of the Secretariat of Agriculture, Livestock, Water Resources, Fisheries and Aquaculture (SAGARPA). Emphasizing that the extracted volume will generate an economic spill directly and indirectly of up to 500 million pesos and locates the jellyfish fishery as the second most important in the State. The jellyfish fishing is carried out in a sustainable manner in the Gulf of California, which generates economic and labor spillage during the months of closures of other species. In 2017 when it had the first 45 thousand tons that were exported to China. The 'cannon ball' jellyfish is exported to China at the beginning, however,the market was opened in Korea, Japan, Indonesia, Thailand, some regions of Europe and the United States, being the only one in Mexico.


Geoduck

Recent introduction to Chinese cuisine, the generous clam is popularly perceived as beneficial for health. The generous clam is consumed generally in thin slices or as sashimi, stirred with vegetables or cooked in Homemade According to an organization dedicated to the promotion of a life healthy, the generous clam is rich in protein that helps maintain a system healthy immune In addition, the generous clam has nutrients that increase the metabolism, which leads to the reduction of inflammation, blood circulation and accumulation of water. There is a saying very common in Chinese that means that one should use something that is similar in form to nourish a specific organ; For example, nuts should be consumed to improve brain function. This way,the exaggerated phallic appearance of the generous clam contributes to the belief that can improve reproductive function.


Industry

Due to its great potential in marine resources, one of the main economic activities of the city is fishing and commercialization, for which it has a large fishing fleet and several processing companies. The main fish and seafood are shrimp, chano, crab, saw and dogfish. The fishing fleet of height that counts is of 125 larger boats and 300 smaller boats for fishing on the riverbank. The volumes of capture have made Sonora occupy the first places at national level, both for national consumption and for export.
Sea food processing is a business growing also, with companies exporting to Asia and US markets.
The industrial activity is made up of 5 seafood processing plants and a crab exporting company. The naval industry consists of 4 workshops that are dedicated to the construction and repair of boats. Likewise and in a related manner there are 6 lathe workshops; that generate more than 1,300 jobs.

Tourism

When visiting the city and its surroundings, Rocky Point offers a variety of activities, from ATVs rides in the dunes, to parasailing , trail walking and horse rides. Depending on your interest with family or friends, you will enjoy your time here.

Commerce

The commercial activity is one of its most important activities, since there are around 706 different businesses, among which companies related to tourism services are contemplated, commerce in the city offers an occupation of 59.2% of the active population. Commerce is subdivided into more than 40 types and is made up of hotels, restaurants, gas stations, workshops, groceries, self-service and artisanal shopping centers.

Living

We have an increasing US community living in town now thanks to the great climate, friendly community and low cost of life compared to US. Mexican law requires foreign citizens to acquire property following federal and local regulations through a trust. In the past years new condominium resorts started to develop, and the commercial activity for this sector has been moving up.

Real State

The real estate sector has been on the rise since the 2000s, and has become an important economic sector due to domestic and foreign investment, which has driven hotel and residential projects, with its economic demand linked to the US and Canadian markets. From 2000 to 2004 this Port already had approximately 2,095 residential homes, 921 condominiums distributed mainly in residential areas such as Plaza Las Glorias, the hotel zone Sandy Beach and Encanto Beach, accounting for a total of 1,953 rooms. In 2005, the economic income of this sector reached 303,929 million pesos, placing Puerto Peñasco in one of the most productive cities in the area. In 2007, 60 projects were presented in the city. real estate construction already registered.
